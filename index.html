Unified Data Platform for Water Utilities

Angular RxJS Material design Typescript AWS

I designed and built the full UX/UI and frontend for Lens, a platform that brings together complex datasets from SCADA systems, GIS layers, wired and wireless sensors, and rainfall models into one intuitive interface.

My work focused on turning heavy, domain-specific hydraulic data into a smooth, fast, and approachable user experience. I developed interactive geospatial views, multi-sensor time-series exploration, drag-and-drop tools, and data-rich components capable of handling millions of datapoints while keeping the interface responsive.

The result is a modern, hardware-agnostic platform that helps utilities understand their networks, validate sensor data, and make better decisions through clear visual storytelling and a friction less workflow.
